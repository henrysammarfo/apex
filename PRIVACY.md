# Apex Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-14

This Privacy Policy explains how Apex ("we", "us", "our") handles data when you use the Apex Telegram bot and related services.

## 1) Scope

This policy applies to:

- The Apex Telegram bot.
- The Apex monitoring/decision/automation services.
- Related dashboard and backend logs used for testing and demo operations.

## 2) Data We Process

Depending on your usage, we may process:

- Telegram identifiers (for example: chat ID, message metadata required to deliver alerts).
- Public blockchain data (wallet addresses, transaction hashes, contract addresses, on-chain events).
- Portfolio telemetry for monitoring and audit (allocation snapshots, drift metrics, execution outcomes).
- Optional integration metadata (for example, HSP reference IDs, NexaID attestation hashes) when those integrations are enabled.
- Technical logs (timestamps, status/error events, service health details).

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data unless you provide it directly in free-form messages.

## 3) How We Use Data

We use data to:

- Deliver Telegram alerts and bot responses.
- Monitor portfolio state and trigger automation rules.
- Record audit trails for decisions and on-chain execution.
- Debug, secure, and improve service reliability.
- Meet hackathon/demo reporting needs.

## 4) Legal Basis

Where applicable, we process data based on:

- Your consent (for optional interactions).
- Performance of requested services.
- Legitimate interests in operating, securing, and improving Apex.

## 5) Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

We may share limited data with infrastructure providers strictly required to run Apex, such as:

- Telegram (message delivery),
- RPC providers / blockchain infrastructure,
- Database/logging providers,
- Hosting providers.

These providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

## 6) Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for operations, auditability, debugging, and demo/reporting purposes.

Retention windows may vary by component. We may delete or anonymize older records when no longer required.

## 7) Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including:

- Environment-based secret management,
- Access controls on operational systems,
- Logging and monitoring for abnormal activity.

No method of transmission or storage is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

## 8) Your Choices

You can:

- Stop using the bot at any time.
- Request deletion of Telegram-linked records we control, subject to legal/operational constraints.
- Rotate wallets and avoid sharing personal information in messages.

To request data deletion or ask privacy questions, contact us via the project repository issues page.

## 9) Children

Apex is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction).

## 10) International Processing

Infrastructure providers may process data in different countries. By using Apex, you understand that cross-border processing may occur.

## 11) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected by revising the "Last updated" date.
